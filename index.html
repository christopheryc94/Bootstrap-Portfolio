About Me

Hi! My name is Christopher Clark and I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona. Growing up in Tucson I attended high school at Sahuaro where I was apart of the school orchestra for all four years and wrestling team for two before a shoulder injury put me out of my last season. Post high school I attended some classes at the University of Arizona but never really knew what I wanted to pursue for a career, which is why I am now currently taking a bootcamp for coding through the University. After this bootcamp, I plan on furthering my programming skillcase by learning both Java and Python.

Through this portfolio, I want to showcase to my viewers the projects I have made and worked on. On my portfolio page itself, the links will take you to the github repository. On every page, you can click on the social media icons to be taken directly to my accounts! If you wish to contact me directly on this site, please select "Contact" on the navigation bar. I look forward to hearing from you!
